WUSTL Makerspace + Machine Shop Training

Rikon Bandsaw Training

Do not cut metal with the bandsaw.

  1. Rotate and slide the fence to your desired position.
  2. Lower the blade guide to 1/4 inch above the part.
  3. Power on Energizer on SafetySpot (blue=idle, green=active, red/flashing=error). MANUALLY (not on SafetySpot) shut off by pressing the button.
  4. Energize the Bandsaw in SafeSpot on the equipment's page.
  5. Turn on Digital Interface.
  6. Set speed (for most wood we recommend 'woods->rip/cross-cut' aka press F1 3 times).
  7. Using both hands, push item through blae to cut it.
  8. Clean up leftover debris with shop-vac.

Cordless Drill/Driver Training

Only use on wood and plastic. If unsure if material is okay, ask staff.

Woods:

  • Plywood
  • Particleboard
  • Medium density fiberboard (MDF)
  • Low density fiberboard (i.e. Masonite)
  • Hardwoods (Maple, Oak)
  • Softwoods (Pine, Basswood, Balsa, Birch)

Plastics:

  • Poly-Vinyl Chloride (PVC)
  • Acetal (i.e. Delrin)
  • Polycarbonate (i.e. Lexan)
  • Acrylic
  • Nylon (3D printed parts)
  • PVA (3D printed parts
