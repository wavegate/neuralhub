Materials

From Sigma-Aldrich (St. Louis, MO):

  • Laminin
  • Polyethyleneimine
  • Hank’s balanced salt solution (HBSS)
  • Collagenase
  • Trypsin
  • Glial-derived neurotrophic factor (GDNF)
  • Uridine
  • 5-fluoro-2-deoxyuridine

From Thermo Fisher Scientific (Waltham, MA):

  • Fetal bovine serum (FBS)
  • Dulbecco’s modified Eagle’s medium-nutrient mixture F-12 (DMEM/F-12)
  • GlutaMAX
  • DNase

Prepare MEAs

  1. Coat the center of each well with PEI (0.1%).

    2. Concentration of supplied PEI is 50 wt.% in H2O. So need to dilute by 500. The goal volume is 100 ul per well. So for 6 wells total we will need 600 ul. So let's make 10ml total. So should be 20 ul PEI in 10 ml H2O. Add 100ul to the center of each well.

  2. Incubate at 37°C and 10% CO2 overnight.
  3. The following morning, rinse three times with sterile double de-ionized water and allow to dry.
  4. Add 10 microliters laminin (20 ug/ml) to center of each well and incubate at 37°C for 2 h. Remove laminin immediately before cell seeding.

    5. Concentration provided is 1.7 mg/ml. Should be thawed and kept at 4-8 degrees C. 1.7/x=0.02. So dilute by 85. If we want 1ml total, add 12 ul.

Primary DRG extraction and culture

  1. Isolate DRGs and place in ice-cold HBSS.
  2. Transfer to hood, and wash with washing medium (40ml HBSS + 0.132 ml 300x stock gentamycin + 0.4ml 100x stock kanamycin).
  3. Transfer to dissociation solution, consisting of collagenase (2 mg/ml) and DNase (0.1 mg/ml) and incubate for 40 min at 37°C.

    4. -1/12/22 Created 2x collagenase solution (4mg/ml) in HBSS; 2x dissociation solution: 4 mg/ml collagenase and 0.2mg/ml DNase. To make 6ml dissociation solution, dissolve 24mg collagenase IV in 6ml HBSS, filter-sterilize, and then add 62ul DNase. The DNase I is supplied by Thermo Fisher in a 20,000 Unit vial with a concentration varying form 50-375 U/ul. The specific activity of DNase I is typically in the range of 10,000-25,000 units/mg. Therefore:

    $$0.1\text{mg DNase I}*\frac{17,500\text{ units}}{1 \text{ mg}}*\frac{1\text{ ul}}{338.5\text{ units}} = 5.17\text{ ul DNase I}$$

    Since we're making 6ml of a 2x solution, we need to multiply this by 12.

    $$5.17\text{ ul DNase I}*2*6=62.04\text{ ul DNase}$$

    Make fresh collagenase solution (4mg/ml). Use ~4ml total 1x dissociation to allow for more tissue movement during digestion. So this means add 2ml collagenase (4mg/ml) to 2ml HBSS (with DRGs). 2ml collagenase at 4mg/ml = 8mg collagenase IV in 2ml HBSS. Filter-sterilize.

  4. After 40 min, add trypsin (0.025%) and incubate for an additional 5 min at 37°C.
  5. Triturate with fire-polished glass Pasteur pipette until solution appears homogeneous.
  6. Pass solution through 70-um nylon filter, and triple the volume with DMEM/F12 + 10% FBS to quench trypsinization.
  7. Centrifuge cells (10 min at 600g) and resuspend in fresh complete medium (DMEM/F-12 + GlutaMAX, 10% FBS, 1% P/S, 5 ng/ml GDNF).

    8. To make 20ml of complete cell medium, we need 20ml DMEM/F12 + 200 ul of GlutaMAX 100X + 2 ml FBS + 200 ul P/S + 100ng. GDNF should be diluted in 0.1% BSA. We have 7.5% BSA in DPBS, so dilute by factor of 75 to make a total of 1ml = 14 ul BSA with 986 ul of DPBS, making 1 ml of 0.1% BSA. The GDNF (total 10ug) was aliquoted into 10 parts, each one with 1ug in 100ul of 0.1% BSA, making concentration 10ug/1ml. So each 1ml of complete medium should have 5 ng, or 1/200 of 100ul. Meaning every 2 ml of media should have 1 ul of GDNF solution.

  8. Seed 5-10 ul containing an estimated 10,000 cells at the center of each MEA device.
  9. Incubate at 37°C and 10% CO2 for 30 min to allow cells to adhere.
  10. Carefully flood each well with 600 ul of fresh, preheated complete cell medium.
  11. Incubate plates at 37°C, 10% CO2, and 95% humidity.
  12. Replace media after the first 24 h and every alternate day afterwards.
  13. After nonneuronal cells reach 90% confluence (DIV 5-7), supplement media with mitotic inhibitors uridine (17.5 ug/ml) and 5-fluoro-2'-deoxyuridine (7.5 ug/ml).

    14. 17.5 ug/ml * 2 ml uridine: in 2ml of solution we want 35 ug 3500ug or 3.5 mg in 2ml, then add 20ul 5-fluoro: in 2ml of solution we want 15 ug, dilute 1500 ug or 1.5mg in 2ml, then add 20ul
