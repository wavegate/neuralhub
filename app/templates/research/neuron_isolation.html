Neuron Isolation

We are following this protocol for the DRG organ culture and this protocol for the DRG neuron isolation.

Clean and trim off excess fibers and connective tissue attached to DRGs with blade.

Put all DRGs in 1.5 ml sterile tube with 1.2 ml of F12 media containing 1.25 mg/ml collagenase IV and incubate for 45 min. Replace with fresh media/collagenase and incubate for another 45 min.

1.2 ml * 1.25 mg/ml = 1.5 mg collagenase IV

Add 2 ml of F12 media containing 0.025% trypsin and incubate for 30 min.

1.25ml of 0.04% trypsin, 0.75 ml of F12

Incubate with 2 ml of F12 media containing 33% FBS for 15 min.

Wash explants 3 times with 2 ml of F12 media and mechanically dissociate. We can use needles of varying sizes.

Filter the dissociated cells through a 0.22 um filter. Centrifuge the filtered cell lysate at (2,500 x g) for 2 min. !!! We don't have 0.22 cell strainer so need to order, BD Falcon 352350

Remove supernatant and resuspend cell pellet in 500 uL of neurobasal media with B27 supplement, P/S, GlutaMAX, and NGF (5 ug/ml).

Plate cells onto laminin-coated cover slides (50 ug/ml).
