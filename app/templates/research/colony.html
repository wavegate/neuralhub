The current transgenic mouse we are working with is the Thy1-GCaMP6s4.3 (https://www.jax.org/strain/024275). Thy1, or CD90, is a T cell marker that is also expressed in neurons. GCaMP6s is an ultrasensitive detector of single neuronal actional potentials and has slow response kinetics.

Dana H, Chen TW, Hu A, et al. Thy1-GCaMP6 transgenic mice for neuronal population imaging in vivo. PLoS One. 2014;9(9):e108697. Published 2014 Sep 24. doi:10.1371/journal.pone.0108697
