Explant

We are following this protocol for the DRG organ culture and this protocol for the DRG neuron isolation.

Put Matrigel into 4 C fridge the day before or four hours before experiment.

After taking out the DRG, place into a Petri dish with sterile, pre-chilled non-phenol red HBSS and transfer to hood. Wash.

Place DRG on coverglass and cover with 60 uL of Matrigel. Allow to polymerize.

Fill with warm RPMI with GlutaMax, Phenol Red with 1% P/S. Culture in incubator.
