Statistics

The topics in AP statistics for high school:

Exploring one-variable data

Variables, categorical variables, tables, graphs, quantitative variables, summary statistics, distributions, normal distribution

Exploring two-variable data

Relationships between variables, correlation, linaer regression, residuals, least squares regression, departures from linearity

Collecting data

Random sampling, experimental design, inference and experiments

Probability

Random and non-random patterns, simulation, mutually exclusive events, conditional probability, random variables, combining random variables, binomial distribution, geometric distribution

Sampling distributions

Normal distribution (revisited), central limit theorem, biased and unbiased point estimates, sampling distributions for sample proportions

Proportions

Confidence interval for a population proportion, p-values, two population proportions

Inference for quantitative data: means

confidence interval, population mean

Inference for categorical data: chi-square

chi-square goodness of fittest, expected counts in two-way tables, chi-square test for homogeneity or independence

Slopes

confidence intervals for slope of a regression model
